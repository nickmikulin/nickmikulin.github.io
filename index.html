MODUL

For years I watched developers, teachers, students, and seniors struggle with presentation tools that are overly complex and clunky. I wanted to make slide building easy and playful for people who need to present but don't have time to fight the software. Together with a technical co-founder, I built the product from scratch — covering user research, design, and UI development with React. Try for free at modul.page ↗

smthmind.com

Challenged myself to code solo and ship a working product over a weekend. Built a clean, minimalist web app for quick reflections using IndexedDB for local-only data storage. Achieved 5th Product of the Day ↗ on Product Hunt and open sourced ↗ it. In 2025 with the help of Claude Code I converted it into a minimalist iOS application and published it on the App Store.
